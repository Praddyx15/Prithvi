Reading Groups & Meet-ups

Ongoing reading groups and study circles for those interested in engaging with psychoanalytic texts, poetry, and related material. These are spaces for serious discussion, not casual book clubs.

Psychoanalysis Reading Group

A group focused on reading foundational and contemporary psychoanalytic texts. Sessions involve close reading, discussion, and exploration of key concepts. Participants are expected to read the assigned material beforehand and come prepared to engage thoughtfully.

Current Text

To be announced. The group selects texts collectively based on shared interests and areas of inquiry.

Format: Monthly meetings, 2 hours per session
Location: Online (video call)
Participation: Limited to small groups for meaningful discussion

Poetry Reading Group

A group dedicated to reading poetry—not as literary criticism, but as a way of attending to language, image, and unconscious resonance. We read slowly, explore what emerges, and consider what poetry makes available that prose cannot.

Poets We Read

Selections vary, but may include classical and contemporary poets across traditions. The focus is on depth, not breadth—staying with a poem long enough for it to unfold.

Format: Bi-monthly meetings, 90 minutes per session
Location: Online or in-person (as feasible)
Participation: Open to those with genuine interest in poetry

No Upcoming Meet-ups Scheduled

Custom Reading Groups

If you have a specific text or topic you would like to explore in a small group setting, custom reading groups can be arranged. This might involve a particular psychoanalytic theorist, a clinical focus, or interdisciplinary work linking psychoanalysis with philosophy, literature, or cultural theory.

For inquiries about custom groups, visit the Special Requests page or get in touch.
