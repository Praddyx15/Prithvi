Special Requests

Custom arrangements for lectures, discussions, collaborations, and other psychoanalytic work tailored to specific needs or contexts.

Custom Lecture Requests

If you have a specific psychoanalytic topic, theorist, or clinical question you would like explored in depth, custom lectures can be arranged. These can be one-on-one sessions or designed for groups, institutions, or academic settings.

Possible topics include:

  • Specific psychoanalytic theorists (Freud, Klein, Lacan, Winnicott, etc.)
  • Clinical concepts (transference, resistance, countertransference, interpretation)
  • Applications to particular fields (literature, film, cultural studies)
  • Introduction to psychoanalytic theory for beginners
  • Advanced seminars for practitioners

Request a Custom Lecture

Group Discussions

For study groups, institutions, or reading circles, facilitated discussions can be arranged around psychoanalytic texts or themes. These are not conventional lectures, but structured conversations that allow participants to engage with difficult material in a supportive setting.

Format: Flexible based on group needs
Duration: 90-120 minutes typical
Delivery: Online or in-person (as feasible)

Arrange a Group Discussion

Academic & Clinical Collaborations

Collaborations with academic institutions, clinical training programs, or research projects are possible. This might involve:

  • Guest lectures for university courses
  • Workshops for clinical training programs
  • Consultation on psychoanalytic research projects
  • Contribution to edited volumes or academic publications
  • Supervision or case consultation for practitioners

If you are affiliated with an institution and have a specific proposal, reach out to discuss possibilities.

Propose a Collaboration

Supervision & Case Consultation

For practicing therapists interested in psychoanalytic perspectives on their clinical work, individual or group supervision can be arranged. This is not technical supervision for credentialing purposes, but a space to think psychoanalytically about cases, therapeutic relationships, and clinical challenges.

Available for:

  • Psychologists and therapists in training
  • Practitioners seeking psychoanalytic perspectives
  • Clinicians working with complex or long-term cases

Inquire About Supervision

How to Make a Request

All special requests begin with a conversation. Reach out via the contact page with details about what you are looking for, and we can discuss whether it is feasible and how it might be structured.
