Consultation & Availability

If you are considering psychoanalytic work, an initial consultation can help determine whether this approach is right for you. This is not a commitment to ongoing therapy—it is a meeting to discuss what brings you here, what you are looking for, and whether this practice is a good fit.

Availability

Sessions are available at the following times. Please reach out to discuss scheduling an initial consultation or ongoing work.

How to Arrange a Consultation

To schedule an initial meeting, please reach out via:

  • Email: psychoanalyticallyseeking@gmail.com
  • Booking platform: topmate.io/prithvibehl
  • Contact form: Contact page

In your initial message, briefly mention what brings you to seek psychoanalytic work. There is no need for detailed information at this stage—the consultation itself is where we can explore whether this approach is right for you.

A Note on Beginning

Starting psychoanalytic work is not a decision to be made lightly. It requires time, consistency, and a willingness to engage with difficult material. The initial consultation is an opportunity to think through whether this is something you want to commit to.

There is no pressure to begin immediately. Take the time you need to consider what you are looking for, and reach out when you feel ready.
