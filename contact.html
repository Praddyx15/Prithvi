Send a Message

Use the form below to send a message directly. Please include what brings you here and what you are looking for. There is no need for extensive detail at this stage.

What to Include in Your Message

When reaching out, briefly mention:

  • What brings you to seek psychoanalytic work (if relevant)
  • Whether you are looking for individual therapy, lectures, groups, or something else
  • Your availability or preferred times (if scheduling a consultation)

You will receive a response within 2-3 business days. If your inquiry is urgent, please mention this in your message.
